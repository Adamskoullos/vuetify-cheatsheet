# Spacing

- `{property}{direction}-{size}`

> Property

- `m`
- `p`

> Direction

- `t` > **top**
- `b` > **bottom**
- `l` > **left**
- `r` > **right**
- `x` > **left & right**
- `y` > **top & bottom**
- `a` > **all sides**

> Size

- `auto`
- `0` > **0**
- `1` > **4px**
- `2` > **8px**
- `3` > **12px**
- `4` > **16px**
- `5` > **20px**
- `6` > **24px**
- `7` > **28px**
- `8` > **32px**
- `9` > **36px**
- `10` > **40px**
- `11` > **44px**
- `12` > **48px**
- `13` > **52px**
- `14` > **56x**
- `15` > **60px**
- `16` > **64px**

sizes can be prefixed with `n` for negative values
